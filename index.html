Who We Are

Caring for Uganda's Most Vulnerable Children Since 2019

Above Uganda Ministries is a Christian charity founded by Katerega Mayanja in Bugiri District, Eastern Uganda. We provide daily meals, safe shelter, education, and medical support to orphaned and vulnerable children aged 0–18 who have nowhere else to turn.

We operate with full transparency — every shilling donated goes directly to the children. No salaries taken from donations. No hidden costs.

Meet the Children

Every child has a story. Here are just two of the lives being transformed through your generosity.

🧒

Buju Shakur

Age 4 · In our care since 2023

At just 4 years old, Buju carries a story that no child should ever have to live — but also a future still full of hope because of care, love, and compassion.

When he first came to us, he was a fragile little boy needing safety, nutrition, and the warmth of a family. Today, Buju is growing under a foundation of love and daily care — discovering what childhood should feel like.

💛 Sponsor Buju
👦

Gilisom Mulyankyupa

Young boy · Rebuilding with dignity

Gilisom is slowly rebuilding his life with dignity, safety, and hope. His journey began with hardship — but today his story is being rewritten through love and care.

Since joining our ministry, Gilisom experiences regular meals, protection, and education. Each day he grows stronger, more confident, and more hopeful about tomorrow.
